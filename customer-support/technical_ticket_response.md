#  Technical Ticket Response (Customer Support – Advanced)

##  Objective

To demonstrate clear, professional, and structured communication when responding to customer-reported technical issues, ensuring both technical accuracy and customer satisfaction.

---

##  Scenario Overview

A customer reports that their endpoint is not visible in the EDR dashboard and they are concerned about lack of protection.

---

##  Customer Ticket (Incoming)

**Subject:** Endpoint Not Showing in Dashboard

Hello Team,
One of our systems is not appearing in the security dashboard, and we are concerned it may not be protected. Kindly assist urgently.

---

##  Response Approach

### Key Principles:

- Acknowledge the issue
- Show ownership
- Communicate clearly (no jargon overload)
- Provide reassurance
- Explain next steps

---

##  Response (Professional)

**Subject:** RE: Endpoint Not Showing in Dashboard

Dear [Client 1],

Thank you for reaching out.

We understand your concern regarding the endpoint not appearing in the security dashboard, and we are currently investigating the issue to ensure your system remains protected.

Based on initial checks, this issue is commonly related to one of the following:
- The security agent may not be running
- Connectivity issues between the endpoint and the platform
- A delay in synchronization with the dashboard

###  What we are doing:

- Verifying the endpoint’s connectivity status
- Checking agent health and service status
- Reviewing logs for any communication errors

###  What we may need from you:

Kindly confirm:
- If the system is powered on and connected to the internet
- If there were any recent updates or changes made to the system

We will provide an update shortly once our investigation is complete.

Rest assured, we are prioritizing this and working to restore full visibility as quickly as possible.

Best regards,
Cybersecurity Support Team

---

##  Follow-Up Response (After Resolution)

**Subject:** RESOLVED: Endpoint Visibility Restored

Dear [Client 1],

We’re pleased to inform you that the issue has been resolved.

The endpoint was not reporting due to a temporary agent service interruption. The agent has now been successfully restarted, and the system is fully visible and protected within the dashboard.

###  Actions Taken:
- Restarted the security agent
- Verified connectivity to the platform
- Confirmed successful telemetry reporting

Please monitor and let us know if you experience any further issues.

Thank you for your patience and cooperation.

Best regards,
Cybersecurity Support Team
